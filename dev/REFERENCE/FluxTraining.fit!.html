FluxTraining.fit!

fit!(learner, n)

Shorthand for fit!(learner, repeat([TrainingPhase(), ValidationPhase()], n)), i.e. a very basic training loop of n epochs of training followed by validation.

fit!(learner, phases)

Fit learner with FluxTraining.Phases.Phases phases. See [./docs/training/basics.md] for more info on the training loop.
