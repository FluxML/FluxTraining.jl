FluxTraining.LogMetrics

LogMetrics(backends...) <: Callback

Callback that logs step and epoch metrics to one or more FluxTraining.LoggerBackends.

See also FluxTraining.LoggerBackend, FluxTraining.Loggables.Loggable, FluxTraining.log_to, TensorBoardBackend

Example:

logcb = LogMetrics(TensorBoardBackend("tblogs"))
Learner(model, data, opt, lossfn, Metrics(accuracy), logcb)
