FluxTraining.Phases.ValidationPhase

TrainingPhase()

A regular validation phase. It iterates over batches in learner.data.validation and performs a forward pass.

Throws the following events: FluxTraining.Events.EpochBegin, FluxTraining.Events.BatchBegin, FluxTraining.Events.LossBegin, FluxTraining.Events.BatchEnd, FluxTraining.Events.EpochEnd.
