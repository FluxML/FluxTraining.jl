Ecosystem

Unlike fastai, FluxTraining focuses on the training part of the deep learning pipeline. Other packages you may find useful are

  • Metalhead.jl and FluxModels.jl for models

  • Augmentor.jl and DataAugmentation.jl for data augmentation

  • DataLoaders.jl for parallel data loading; and

  • DLDatasets.jl for datasets
